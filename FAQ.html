We have a fixed process of building a PC. Once you finalize the build (whether a custom rig or Pre-built ripper PC) and we receive your payment, the parts are placed for order. Getting hold of these parts depends on market availability and other factors. However, in most cases we build and ship the same within 15 days. You will be provided constant updates on the progress of assembly.

Computer PC parts market is dynamic in nature with prices fluctuating like gold. For e.g. you may get a RTX 3060 Ti for 42K today and come after 10 days, the price could be 40K. Prices are all governed by market availability and demand. Hence, we prefer giving our customers the best price for the parts and the entire build based on current market price.

Refunds or cancellations are only allowed within 42 hours of placing the order. We call this the buffer time. We offer no cancellations on the orders beyond that as we start building your rig from that point of time. We also offer parts replacement if it turns out to be a faulty one i.e. dead on arrival.
